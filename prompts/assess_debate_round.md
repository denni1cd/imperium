# Debate Round Assessment

As Seneschal, inspect the direct challenge-response exchanges from the completed round.

Continue only when a high- or critical-materiality issue remains unresolved and another specific exchange could change adjudication, clarify decision-critical evidence, or expose a genuinely new frame. Stop when remaining disagreement is already understood, merely repeated, or should instead be preserved as a minority objection.

Do not manufacture another round to create the appearance of debate. Return exactly one `ContinuationDecision`.
